## Overview

Percona is an open source database software, support, and services company headquartered in Raleigh/Durham, North Carolina. The company develops freely available database software for MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, and provides expert support, consulting, and operational services for those databases plus Valkey, Redis, and MariaDB. Percona's software is 100% compatible with upstream community versions but includes enterprise-grade features that are typically only available in proprietary editions.

---

## Founding and history

- Founded: August 2006
- Founders: Peter Zaitsev and Vadim Tkachenko
- Origin: Both founders left MySQL AB in 2006 to start Percona as a two-person consultancy focused on MySQL performance
- Grew from MySQL consulting into a multi-database software and services company
- The founders co-authored "High Performance MySQL" (3rd edition, O'Reilly) with Baron Schwartz
- 2026 marks Percona's 20th year in operation

## Mission and positioning

**Mission statement (from percona.com):** "Percona is on a mission to provide a unified approach to open source database software, support, and services."

**Founding belief:** "Everyone should have access to scalable, secure, efficient, and resilient database software features without inflated fees or vendor lock-in."

**Positioning statement:** "At Percona, we make your database, and the applications that depend on it, better."

**Brand tagline:** "Databases run better with Percona."

**Three freedoms (brand pillars):**
1. Freedom to choose (no vendor lock-in)
2. Freedom to create (open source, customizable)
3. Freedom to make a difference (innovate and grow)

**Key positioning themes under Peter Farkas:**
- Sharpening focus on hands-on expert services and open source community leadership
- Multi-database, open source-first model
- One-stop shop for open source database technology across MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Valkey/Redis

## Funding and ownership

- Ownership: Private. **Fully bootstrapped and independent. No VC ownership.**
- Funding: Minimal external funding ($1M total over 1 round per public sources)
- Investor: Gideon Hixon Fund
- Acquisition: Tokutek (acquired April 2015, TokuDB storage engine technology)
- Percona is profitable from services revenue (all software is freely available)
- **"Independent by design"** is a key messaging pillar: no risk of VC-driven licensing changes or vendor lock-in surprises. This independence means Percona's advice is based on technical merit, not product quotas or platform lock-in.
