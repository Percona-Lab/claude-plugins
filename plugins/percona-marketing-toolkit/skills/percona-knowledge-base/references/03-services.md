## Services overview

Percona's revenue comes from services and support, not software licensing. All Percona software is freely available. The services portfolio helps organizations run, manage, and optimize open source databases in production.

**Current services taxonomy:**

| Service name | What it is | One-line description |
|---|---|---|
| Expert Support | Reactive support | 24x7 incident and performance help from database engineers |
| ExpertOps | Proactive management | Ongoing database operations management by Percona engineers |
| Expert Consulting and Services | Project-based work | Custom-scoped consulting for complex database projects |
| Percona Packages | Fixed-scope offerings | Structured consulting and support packages for specific use cases |
| Training | Education | Instructor-led, hands-on database training |

## Expert Support

**What it is:** Reactive support and expert guidance for teams that operate their own databases. 24x7x365 incident response, troubleshooting, and advisory guidance.

**Positioning:** Expert Support is *reactive and advisory*: customers own execution, and Percona engineers respond when issues or questions arise.

**What it solves:** Outages and urgent incidents, replication or high availability failures, performance regressions, uncertain upgrade/migration plans, and hard architectural questions.

**Who buys it:** Teams with capable DBAs or SREs who manage their own databases but want reliable expert escalation for production issues, architectural questions, and review of planned changes.

**Databases covered:** MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Valkey/Redis, and MariaDB

**What's included:**
- 24x7x365 access to senior database engineers
- Incident response and troubleshooting (SLA-backed)
- Performance tuning advisory and review
- Upgrade and migration plan review
- Configuration review and validation
- Best-practice guidance on HA, DR, scaling

**Support tiers and SLAs:**

| Tier | Severity 1 response time |
|---|---|
| Advanced Support | 30 minutes |
| Premium Support | 15 minutes |

- Severity 1 incidents receive Root Cause Analysis (RCA) within five business days
- Incident management follows a defined escalation path
- Optional personalized support: named engineers, embedded communication channels, and regular operational reviews (when included in agreement)

**Key differentiator:** Percona's support engineers are upstream-level database experts who contribute to and deeply understand the code for MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Valkey, and Redis. These are the specialists customers call when issues exceed what cloud vendors, DBaaS platforms, or typical consultancies can resolve. Because Percona has no proprietary licensing business, support advice is based on technical merit, not product quotas.

**What Expert Support does NOT do:**
- Does not perform hands-on operations (that's ExpertOps)
- Does not execute migrations or complex project work (that's Expert Consulting)
- Does not provide 24x7 monitoring or proactive management (that's ExpertOps)

## ExpertOps

**What it is:** Proactive, hands-on database management partnership. Percona engineers perform work directly: continuous monitoring, tuning, patching, automation, backup validation, and routine operational tasks.

**Previous name:** Managed Services. ("Managed Services" is the old name. The name was changed because "managed services" is often used to describe SaaS offerings. Replace in all new copy.)

**Positioning:** ExpertOps is *proactive and operational*: a flexible partnership where Percona engineers handle the parts of day-to-day database operations customers want support with, ranging from full operational ownership to shared workflows or targeted help with high-risk tasks.

**What it solves:** Operational noise and alert fatigue, manual or inconsistent scaling, maintenance and tuning, downtime from preventable issues, operational backlog, staffing limitations, and "day two" operational risk after migrations or upgrades.

**Who buys it:** Teams with limited staff or time to manage databases, companies that need 24x7 operational coverage, organizations that want to prevent incidents instead of reacting to them, environments where uptime is business-critical.

**What's included:**
- 24x7 monitoring and alerting (proactive, Percona often sees issues before customers do)
- Proactive performance optimization and query tuning
- Backup management and validation
- Patching and upgrade management
- Automation through PMM, Operators, and automation frameworks
- Routine operational tasks
- When scoped and hours are available, can execute defined changes (upgrades, migrations)

**Key differentiator:** Unlike "black box" managed services, Percona's ExpertOps gives customers full visibility into what's being done and why. Customers retain control and insight. (Consistent theme in testimonials: CouponCabin, Protectall, Optimum Instruments chose Percona specifically because they didn't want to cede visibility.)

**Outcomes ExpertOps delivers:** Proactive issue prevention, stable and predictable performance, consistent tuning and maintenance, reduced operational burden on engineering teams, and faster incident response from engineers already familiar with the environment.

**What ExpertOps does NOT do:**
- Does not handle complex project work like architecture modernization (that's Expert Consulting)
- Does not provide team enablement or training (that's Expert Consulting)

## Expert Consulting and Services

**What it is:** Project-based execution and specialized expertise for major architectural changes, deep technical analysis, and complex edge-case resolution. Delivered as defined projects with clear deliverables and predictable outcomes.

**Positioning:** Expert Consulting and Services are *proactive and project-based*: Percona experts help teams plan and execute complex, high-impact work at the level of involvement the customer needs.

**What it solves:** Complex migrations or upgrades, scaling and performance bottlenecks, architecture uncertainty or high-impact design decisions, security or compliance gaps, unclear patterns in query performance or storage behavior.

**Who buys it:** Teams preparing for major changes, organizations with strong internal talent that need expert help for the hardest or highest-risk work, companies modernizing architectures or preparing for audits.

**Typical engagements:**
- Database Performance Audit (health check, configuration review, optimization recommendations)
- Migration planning and execution (Oracle to PostgreSQL, MySQL Enterprise to Percona Server, version upgrades, EOL migrations)
- Architecture design and review (including modernization blueprinting)
- High-availability and DR design (resilience architecture)
- Deep performance tuning (schema, query, configuration, storage layer)
- Security assessments and compliance remediation roadmaps
- Audit preparation and readiness
- Cloud migration advisory
- Team enablement and training workshops
- Deploying Operators, EOL upgrades, major version shifts (often front-loaded before ExpertOps takes over)

**Outcomes:** Expert-built migration or upgrade plans, faster performance through tuning, secure and well-architected environments, clear recommendations and action plans, reduced risk during critical transitions.

**Key differentiator:** Percona consultants handle "the hard, high-skill, complex work" that internal teams can't solve alone. They are hands-on practitioners who design solutions and execute critical tasks, not slide-deck consultants. Engagements are offered with transparent estimates and clear deliverables.

## Percona Packages (Solution Bundles)

**What it is:** A set of focused, fixed-scope solution bundles designed to deliver fast, measurable outcomes. These represent the most common, high-impact use cases and combine Expert Support, ExpertOps, and Expert Consulting modules. Customers can also tailor any engagement by adding modules as needed.

**Current bundles:**

### Performance Optimization Bundle
- **For:** Teams needing measurable performance gains or relief from chronic performance issues
- **Outcomes:** Better workload performance, reduced latency and bottlenecks, stabilized systems under load
- **Includes:** Performance tuning engagement (Consulting), proactive health assessment (Consulting), 6 months of Advanced Support, optional add-ons (architecture review or training)

### QuickStart Bundle
- **For:** New customers validating Percona or quickly stabilizing a smaller deployment
- **Outcomes:** Rapid environment stabilization, baseline health and readiness, faster time-to-value
- **Includes:** Targeted health check or tuning (Consulting), 3 months of Advanced Support
- **Scope:** Limited to one technology and 10 or fewer servers

### AI Readiness Bundle
- **For:** Teams adopting vector search or preparing PostgreSQL for AI-driven applications
- **Outcomes:** PostgreSQL readiness for vector search and AI workloads, better observability, indexing, and concurrency
- **Includes:** Setup and tuning of pgvector, pgvectorscale, pg_stat_kcache, pg_wait_sampling; query pattern optimization; observability and performance enhancements
