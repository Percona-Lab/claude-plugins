## How Percona makes money

Percona's software is freely available. Revenue comes entirely from services:

1. **Expert Support** (subscription): Ongoing 24x7 support contracts
2. **ExpertOps** (subscription): Ongoing managed database operations
3. **Expert Consulting and Services** (project-based): Fixed-scope engagements
4. **Percona Packages** (fixed-scope): Structured offerings (Quickstart, AI Readiness)
5. **Training** (per-engagement): Instructor-led database training

The business model is: give away the software, build a massive user base, convert a percentage into paying support/services customers.

## Sales motion

> **TODO: Confirm from internal sales team. The following is inferred from public information and case studies.**

**Typical journey:**
1. **Discovery:** Developer or DBA downloads Percona software (PMM, Percona Server, XtraBackup, Toolkit)
2. **Adoption:** Team uses Percona software in development, then production
3. **Need trigger:** Production incident, scaling challenge, compliance requirement, or migration project
4. **Engagement:** Team contacts Percona for support, consulting, or managed services
5. **Expansion:** Initial engagement expands to additional databases, services, or teams

**Common entry points:**
- Production incident or performance crisis (leads to Expert Support)
- Migration project (Oracle to PostgreSQL, MySQL Enterprise to Percona Server) leads to Consulting
- Operational overhead (leads to ExpertOps)
- Compliance/audit requirement (leads to Consulting or Pro Builds)

**Named account approach (ABM):**
- For enterprise targets, Percona uses account-based marketing with research, account plans, and personalized outreach (see percona-account-plan skill)

## Free-to-paid journey

| Stage | What the user does | Percona's opportunity |
|---|---|---|
| Download | Downloads Percona Server, PMM, Toolkit, or XtraBackup | Build awareness, capture lead |
| Evaluate | Tests in development environment | Provide documentation, community support |
| Deploy | Runs in production | Offer Expert Support for peace of mind |
| Scale | Grows production deployment | Offer ExpertOps, consulting for optimization |
| Enterprise | Multi-database, multi-team | Expand to full enterprise engagement |

Percona reports 10M+ downloads of its open source projects (as of 2021). Conversion rate from download to paid customer is not publicly available.
